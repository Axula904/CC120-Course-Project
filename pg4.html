What Can Be Done?

Steps to be taken

In the pursuit of better immigration rights, the obvious and easy to say answer of immigration policy reform gets tossed around a lot, however, as stated previously this is a lot easier said than done. Currently the Build Back Better Bill, proposed by the Biden administration, would work to improve the current conditions for undocumented individuals, however, it would unfortunately not lead to an easier path to legal citizenship. While it can be useful to push for this, this alone does not come fast enough nor does it accomplish enough. Active protests and resistance towards current immigration policies are just as necessary as ever to work towards a better future.

What can be done actively now is more protests surrounding current immigration policy as well as different forms of ICE resistance. One popular method of ICE resistance is by calling and reporting certain numbers regarding ICE activity in a certain area to prepare individuals. Another action that can be taken to resist is by education people on their rights, even as an undocumented individual, so that one can resist against any human rights violations that may, and sadly do, occur in the hands of ICE.
