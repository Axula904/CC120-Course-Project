Who am I?

Hello everyone! I am Axel, obviously, a First Year student at Colorado College. I have a passion for running, cooking, and web development. From sticking to my love and running, through thick and thin, I am lucky enough to be able to say that I get to represent Colorado College as a Varsity Collegiate athlete on the Cross Country and Track and Field teams, I have personal Cross country PRs of; 16:29 for the 5K and 26:58 for the 8K, along with Track PRs of; 2:04 in the 800M, 4:39 in the 1600M, and 10:09 in the 3200M.
