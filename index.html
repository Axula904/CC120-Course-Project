The Story

The United States has long been depicted as the land of opportunity through the pursuit of the “American Dream”. However, the achievement of the “American Dream” is far easier said than done. Rather than being a nation where this “American Dream” is achievable, the United States has been a nation deeply rooted in systemic racism designed to make the lives of others more difficult. In recent years it feels as if the divide between left and right has gotten even further apart and more distinct than ever before. And yet, despite all of the oppressive and hateful systems that are in place millions of people still flock to the United States in a hope that they can build a life here, but not only do immigrants come to the United States for themselves, but they also come and help the United States itself. It has become far too easy for people to forget how dependent the United States is on the immigrant population here.
